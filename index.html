    I'm a Product Consultant turned Software Engineer who loves building systems that solve real problems. For the past three years, I've worked at SSandC as a Consultant on the Salentica Engage product, helping clients design workflows, streamline data, and translate business needs into technical solutions.

    Along the way, I realized my favorite part of the job wasn't just advising, it was building. That pushed me into Software Development in my spare time, where I now focus on creating full-stack applications using Java, Spring Boot, JavaScript, and React, among other technologies.

    I've built projects like Finance Hub, a CRM platform with secure authentication and a clean API layer, and Dos Santos Swim School, a full-stack application that blends branding, UI design, and backend logic. Each project reflects the same mindset I bring to my consulting work: clarity, reliability, and thoughtful user experience.

    In addition to my project work, I have been fortunate to receive ongoing mentorship from the Application Development Manager at SSandC, which has helped me refine my engineering approach and deepen my understanding of real-world software development practices.

    Right now, I'm focused on deepening my engineering foundations, shipping polished projects, and transitioning into a full-time software engineering role where I can combine technical depth with product intuition.
